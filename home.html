Development of Prediction Model for PMGSY Roads

Advanced predictive analytics for rural road maintenance planning using Markov Chain modeling

About Our Project

Our project focuses on developing a software tool for predicting the condition of rural roads under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) program. Using Markov Chain modeling, we've created a predictive system that helps optimize road maintenance planning and resource allocation.

Project Objectives

  • Assess the condition of PMGSY roads in Shivamogga district through visual surveys and PCI rating
  • Predict future road conditions using Markov Chain modeling
  • Develop a decision support system for maintenance planning
  • Optimize resource allocation for rural road maintenance
  • Extend the serviceable life of rural road infrastructure

Key Features

  • Pavement Condition Index (PCI) assessment tool
  • Markov Chain-based deterioration prediction
  • 10-year road condition forecasting
  • Visualization of road condition trends
  • Maintenance recommendation system
  • Budget estimation for rehabilitation

Why This Project Matters

Rural road networks are critical for economic development and social welfare in India. The PMGSY program has significantly improved rural connectivity, but maintaining these roads efficiently is challenging due to limited resources. Our predictive model helps:

  • Identify roads needing immediate attention
  • Plan maintenance activities proactively
  • Optimize limited maintenance budgets
  • Extend the lifespan of rural roads
  • Improve safety and ride quality
  • Support data-driven decision making

With our tool, decision-makers can implement the right treatment at the right time, reducing long-term maintenance costs while maximizing road performance.
